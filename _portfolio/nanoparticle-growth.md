This work focuses on understanding nanoparticle growth and sintering mechanisms through molecular dynamics simulations. The research investigates how gas-phase interactions influence the sintering process, which is critical for understanding particle formation in various environments.

Key aspects of this research include:
- Molecular dynamics simulations of nanoparticle systems
- Analysis of sintering kinetics with gas-phase interactions
- Understanding particle growth mechanisms
- Applications in cryopreservation and material science
